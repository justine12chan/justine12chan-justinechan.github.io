The Three Regiment on Attack On Titan

Military Police

The elite guards responsible for maintaining order inside the walls and protecting royalty.

Survey Corps

The brave explorers beyond the walls, tasked with scouting Titans and reclaiming land.

Garrison Regiment

The defenders of the walls, responsible for fortifications and protecting towns.

Characters

Eren Yeager

Eren Yeager is the main protagonist of Attack on Titan. Initially driven by a desire for freedom and revenge against the Titans, his journey leads him to uncover deep secrets about the world, the Titans, and himself. Eren’s power as the Founding Titan becomes a key element in the battle for humanity’s survival.

Levi Ackerman

Known as humanity’s strongest soldier, Levi is a captain in the Survey Corps. Calm, strategic, and deadly in combat, Levi is deeply respected by his comrades. Despite his stoic demeanor, he shows profound loyalty and care for those he leads.

Mikasa Ackerman

Mikasa is Eren’s adoptive sister and one of the most skilled soldiers in the series. Her devotion to Eren drives many of her actions, but she also represents strength, resilience, and the emotional core of the main group of characters. As a member of the Ackerman clan, she has heightened combat abilities.

Armin Arlert

Armin is one of Eren’s closest childhood friends and a strategic genius. Despite his initial lack of physical strength, his intelligence and compassion make him a key member of the team. He eventually inherits the power of the Colossal Titan, which adds a new dimension to his character and responsibilities.

Hange Zoë

Hange is a brilliant scientist and former Squad Leader who becomes Commander of the Survey Corps. Known for their eccentricity and obsession with Titans, Hange provides comic relief and scientific insight. Beneath their quirky demeanor lies a fierce dedication to humanity’s survival and the pursuit of truth.
